🌱 How to Care for Newly Planted Trees

The first year is critical for young trees. Proper watering, mulching, and monitoring ensure strong root development.

  • Water deeply 1–2 times per week
  • Apply mulch to retain moisture
  • Protect from strong winds and extreme heat
  • Inspect regularly for pests or disease

🌳 Seasonal Tree Maintenance

Trees require different care throughout the year to remain healthy.

  • Spring: Fertilize and check for new growth
  • Summer: Monitor watering and pest activity
  • Autumn: Remove fallen leaves and prune lightly
  • Winter: Protect young trees from frost damage

🌿 Indoor Plant Care Basics

Indoor plants improve air quality and add beauty to your space. Follow these essentials for thriving indoor greenery:

  • Provide adequate natural or indirect light
  • Use well-draining potting soil
  • Avoid overwatering
  • Rotate plants for even growth

🍎 Fruit Tree Care Tips

Fruit trees need proper pruning, feeding, and pollination to produce abundant harvests.

  • Prune annually to improve air circulation
  • Fertilize during the growing season
  • Ensure proper spacing between trees
  • Harvest fruit promptly when ripe
